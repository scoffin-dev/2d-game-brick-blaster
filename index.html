GAME RULES

  • Move the paddle using left & right arrow keys.
  • Hitting the floor restarts you at center if you have more lives.
  • Destroy a brick: +1 point
  • Hit the floor: -1 life
  • Destroy all bricks to win the game.
  • Lose all lives to lose the game.
